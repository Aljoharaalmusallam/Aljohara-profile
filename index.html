About Me

Biomedical Engineering graduate from King Saud University (First Class Honors, GPA 4.82/5.00) with a year-long clinical internship at National Guard Health Affairs (NGHA), Riyadh.

My internship spanned medical device maintenance, troubleshooting and calibration using Fluke biomedical test equipment, CMMS operations (AssetPlus), and exposure to hospital systems integration — including HL7 workflows and middleware across Imaging, Cardiac, OR, Laboratory, and General Biomedical Engineering departments.

I am interested in the intersection of clinical engineering and healthcare IT — specifically how devices, data standards, and integration platforms combine to deliver safer, smarter care environments.
